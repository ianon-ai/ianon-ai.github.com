# Welcome to ianon

> **ianon** / eye-a-non / *coined term*  
> The future of private AI access.

ianon represents the next step in how AI should be accessed by everyone.

The AI industry has fallen into its default mode: models hosted in the cloud by provider companies, where your personal metadata can become just as important as your prompts.

These systems are often designed to build a unique digital profile around you, learning your habits, preferences, behaviour, and identity over time.

But what if you do not want that?

ianon is for those of us who question that default.

---

## Private AI access via ianon

At its core, ianon is a local, open-source LLM frontend built for anonymity.

It lets you connect multiple AI providers from one desktop app, including GPT, Claude, Venice, and more. Some providers may come bundled with the app, while others can be added using your own API keys.

All requests are routed through the [Anyone Network](#privacy/secure-routing), a free-to-use onion routing network built for private internet access.

Using Anyone and the routing logic built into ianon, your identity can be continually rotated. This helps change the IP address, country, and device metadata associated with your requests.

ianon is also being built to support more precise identity rotation over time, including different countries for different models, changing APIs, and more advanced privacy controls.

---

## Manage your AI tools in one place

ianon also makes it easier to manage all your LLMs from one interface.

You can:

- Add and manage multiple AI providers
- Switch between models easily
- Send the same prompt to multiple providers
- Compare responses side by side
- Use routing logic designed to make AI access more private
- Keep token costs more manageable by choosing the right model for each task

ianon is an evolving product powered by community, privacy-first infrastructure, and the associated crypto token.
